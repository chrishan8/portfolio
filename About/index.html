About Me

I currently work a 9 to 5 as a web developer at Accenture Technology, and I’ve been helping a Fortune 500 client redesign, refactor, and overhaul their B2B eCommerce storefronts used by retail businesses to order products from the client’s manufacturers. Outside of my day job, I run my own freelance business, work on a myriad of unfinished side projects, and learn new technologies. However, when I’m feeling particularly lazy, I like to order takeout from a nice restaurant, grab a six-pack, and binge-watch sci-fi shows. There’s no better feeling in this world than creating beautiful, efficient software and convincing people that I’m a wizard. I’m currently accepting freelance work, so if you’re in need of a professional nerd, then don’t hesitate to get in touch.
